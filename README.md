# 🌍 Green Destinations HR Analytics Dashboard (Tableau)

> 📊 *Analyzing employee attrition, satisfaction, and workforce trends to drive better HR decisions*

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project focuses on building an **interactive HR analytics dashboard** for Green Destinations using Tableau.

The dashboard provides insights into **employee attrition, job satisfaction, demographics, and work-life balance**, helping HR teams make data-driven decisions to improve retention and workforce performance.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

✔ Analyze employee attrition patterns
✔ Identify factors influencing employee turnover
✔ Understand workforce demographics
✔ Track job satisfaction and work-life balance
✔ Improve employee retention strategies

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

### 🔹 Key KPIs

* 👥 Total Employees
* 🚪 Attrition Count & Attrition Rate (%)
* 💰 Average Monthly Income
* ⭐ Job Satisfaction Score

## 📈 Key Insights

💡 **Attrition Trends**

* Higher attrition observed in specific departments and job roles

💡 **Salary Impact**

* Employees with lower income show higher attrition rates

💡 **Work-Life Balance**

* Poor work-life balance correlates with higher turnover

💡 **Experience Factor**

* Employees with fewer years at the company are more likely to leave

💡 **Job Satisfaction**

* Lower satisfaction levels directly impact retention
